# Positioning Brief: Keyword Custom Audiences

## Target Persona

**Primary: Pharmaceutical Marketing Directors**
- Title: VP Marketing, Director of Digital Marketing, Media Buying Director
- Company: Pharma companies ($1B+ revenue), biotech firms, specialty pharmaceuticals
- Pain points: 
  - HCP (Healthcare Provider) targeting restrictions on traditional platforms
  - Need privacy-compliant ways to reach physicians and patients
  - Want intent-based audiences, not demographic guessing
  - Compliance requirements (HIPAA, FDA) make data sourcing complex

**Secondary: Healthcare Marketing Agencies**
- Type: Agencies specializing in pharma/healthcare (e.g., CDM, Ogilvy Health, Harrison & Star)
- Pain points:
  - Need differentiated data products for pharma clients
  - Struggle with audience quality and verification
  - Want turnkey audience solutions, not raw data wrangling
  - Need to prove ROI with precise targeting

**Tertiary: Healthcare MarTech Partners**
- Type: CRM platforms, data onboarding services, identity resolution providers
- Pain points:
  - Need intent signal layers to enrich their tech stack
  - Want API-accessible audiences for integration
  - Looking for differentiated data partnerships

## Pain Points Addressed

1. **Regulatory Complexity**: Pre-built, compliant audience segments for regulated industries
2. **Data Sourcing**: No need to manage multiple vendor relationships
3. **Audience Quality**: Intent-based > demographic targeting
4. **Time to Market**: Pre-made audiences delivered in 24-48 hours
5. **Competitive Advantage**: Keyword-level intent signals competitors don't have

## Competitive Positioning

**vs. Traditional Data Brokers (LiveRamp, Acxiom)**
- We specialize in INTENT (not just identity)
- Faster turnaround (days vs. weeks)
- Keyword-level granularity they can't match

**vs. Platform Native Targeting (LinkedIn, Meta)**
- Off-platform reach (email, programmatic, direct mail)
- Keyword search intent signals
- Not limited by platform's HCP targeting restrictions

**vs. Other Intent Providers (Bombora, Demandbase)**
- Purpose-built for healthcare/pharma compliance
- Direct audience delivery (not just scores)
- Keyword specificity vs. topic-level only

## Recommended Starting Points

1. **"Healthcare Provider (HCP) Targeting"** — Reaching doctors, nurses, specialists
2. **"Pharmaceutical Marketing"** — Intent signals from pharma marketers themselves
3. **"Patient Journey Targeting"** — Condition-specific audiences (diabetes, oncology, etc.)
4. **"Healthcare Data Partnerships"** — MarTech providers seeking data layers
