## Executive Summary

**Why Pharma Worked**: High CAC + Platform restrictions + Proven intent signals ("buy tirzepatide") = Perfect fit for kwca

**The Pattern to Replicate**:
1. **High customer acquisition cost** (CAC $500-$5,000+)
2. **Platform targeting restrictions** (compliance, competition, sensitivity)
3. **Clear intent signals** (specific search terms indicate purchase readiness)
4. **Large addressable market** (enough search volume to build audiences)
5. **Prohibited or limited on native platform targeting**
