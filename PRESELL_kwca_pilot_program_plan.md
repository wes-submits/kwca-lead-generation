# Presell Value Plan: The kwca Pilot Program
## Productizing First-Touch Value Delivery

---

## Executive Summary

**The Strategy**: Instead of *telling* prospects what kwca does, *show* them by running a pilot on their own audience data—delivered within 48 hours of first touch.

**The Hook**: "Let us analyze your current audience and show you exactly what you're missing."

**The Deliverable**: A personalized **Audience Intelligence Report** revealing intent gaps, competitive blind spots, and enrichment opportunities unique to their business.

**The Outcome**: Prospects see tangible value before buying, reducing sales cycle and increasing close rates.

## THE PRES VALUE OFFER

### What It Is

A **free 48-hour pilot** where kwca:

1. **Analyzes** the prospect's existing audience (or lookalike seed)

3. **Identifies** high-intent segments they're missing
4. **Delivers** a personalized report with actionable insights
