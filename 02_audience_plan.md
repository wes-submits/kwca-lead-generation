## Customer Profile

### Demographics

| Attribute | Profile |
|-----------|---------|
| **Age Range** | 35-55 (decision makers), 28-40 (practitioners) |
| **Job Titles** | VP Marketing, Director of Digital Marketing, Media Director, Account Director, Marketing Manager, Data Strategy Lead |
| **Company Size** | 200-10,000+ employees (enterprise pharma, mid-market agencies) |
| **Industries** | Pharmaceuticals, Biotechnology, Marketing Agencies (healthcare focus), Health Tech/MarTech |
| **Location** | US primary (NY, NJ, CA, MA, IL, NC pharma hubs) |
| **Budget Authority** | $50K-$2M+ annual media/data spend |
| **Education** | Bachelor's+ (MBA common among VPs/Directors) |

### Psychographics

**Primary Goals:**
- Reach HCPs (Healthcare Providers) with precision
- Improve ROAS on pharmaceutical campaigns
- Access privacy-compliant audience data
- Differentiate from competitors with intent signals

**Core Fears:**
- HIPAA/FDA compliance violations
- Wasted ad spend on low-quality audiences
- Platform restrictions (LinkedIn/Meta HCP targeting limits)
- Falling behind competitors using better data
- Legal liability from improper patient targeting

**Values & Beliefs:**
- Data-driven decision making
- Compliance-first approach
- ROI accountability
- Innovation in regulated industries
- Partnership over vendor relationships

**Media Consumption:**
- LinkedIn (primary professional)
- Industry pubs: MM+M, FiercePharma, Endpoints News
- Podcasts: Pharma Marketing Podcast, Digital Health Interviews
- Events: DTC National, MM+M Transform, HLTH

### Pain Points

1. **Platform Limitations**: LinkedIn and Meta heavily restrict HCP targeting; need alternative channels
2. **Data Quality Uncertainty**: Hard to verify if audience data is accurate, recent, and compliant
3. **Vendor Management Fatigue**: Managing 5-10 data vendors is a full-time job
4. **Compliance Complexity**: Healthcare data has unique regulatory requirements most vendors don't understand
5. **Speed to Market**: Traditional data sourcing takes weeks; campaigns need to launch in days
6. **Attribution Gaps**: Hard to connect audience data to actual conversions in pharma (long patient journeys)
7. **Audience Overlap**: Concern that competitors are using the same data sources

### Decision Triggers

**External Events:**
- New drug approval (FDA clearance)
- Competitor campaign launch
- Platform policy changes (Meta/LinkedIn targeting restrictions)
- Data vendor acquisition or shutdown

**Internal Events:**
- New product/service launch
- Quarterly planning cycles
- Agency review/RFP process
- New marketing leadership hire
- Budget increase for digital/precision marketing

**Seasonal Factors:**
- Q4 planning (start in Q3)
- Conference season (DTC National in spring, HLTH in fall)
- Fiscal year alignment (varies by company)
