# MIXED AUDIENCE: How to Choose Positioning

When prospect's channel mix is unclear, use discovery questions:

**Question 1**: "What's your primary customer acquisition channel right now?"
- If "Facebook/Instagram" → Use Positioning 1
- If "Programmatic/CTV/Display" → Use Positioning 2
- If "Multi-channel" → Ask about biggest challenge

**Question 2**: "Where are you seeing the biggest efficiency challenges?"
- If "iOS14/Facebook" → Positioning 1
- If "CTV targeting/cookie deprecation" → Positioning 2

**Question 3**: "What's your annual media spend?"
- Under $1M → Likely Facebook-heavy → Positioning 1
- Over $5M → Likely programmatic/CTV → Positioning 2

# TONE & STYLE GUIDE

## Voice
- **Direct**: No corporate fluff
- **Empathetic**: "Most marketers I talk to say..."
- **Confident**: Not arrogant, but certain of value
- **Conversational**: Write like you talk

## What to Avoid
- ❌ "I'd love to pick your brain"
- ❌ "Just checking in"
- ❌ "Hope you're doing well" (wasted words)
- ❌ Feature dumps instead of outcomes
- ❌ Vague CTAs like "Let me know if you're interested"

## What to Include
- ✅ Specific pain points ("degraded lookalikes")
- ✅ Concrete numbers ("34% lower CAC")
- ✅ Clear next step ("Worth 15 mins?")
- ✅ Easy outs ("If not a priority, no worries")
- ✅ Social proof from similar companies
